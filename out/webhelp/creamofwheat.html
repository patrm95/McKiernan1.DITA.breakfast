Indian Cream of Wheat

Might not look like much, but its a great way to start the day

An easy five minute recipe needing only a stove top, a sauce pan, a whisk and serving bowls

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup whole grain of Cream of Wheat

  • 2 cups Coconut Milk

  • 1 tbsp. rose water

  • 1 tbsp. pistachios (finely chopped)
  • ½ tsp. cardamom
  • 3 tablespoons of maple syrup, brown sugar or 3 packets of Stevia

Recipe

  1. In saucepan, bring coconut milk to a boil, watching it carefully to prevent it from boiling over.
  2. Gradually add whole grain cream of wheat, rosewater, ½ cup of pistachios, cardamom and sugar stirring constantly with wire whisk until well blended.
  3. Whisk without stopping so that no lumps form.
  4. Reduce heat to Low uncovered for 3 minutes or until thickened, stirring frequently.
  5. Transfer to serving bowls, and garnish with remaining pistachios before serving.
