Toast with Refried Beans and Avacado

A delcious vegan recipe that only takes five minutes of your time

The only tools this recipe requires is toaster and normal silverwear, such as a fork and knife

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sandwich bread
  • 1 cup homemade or store-bought vegan refried beans
  • 1 avocado, thinly sliced
  • A few slivers white onion (users descretion)
  • Coarse sea salt

Recipe

  1. Toast bread to desired level of doneness.
  2. Top with refried beans and avocado (mash with a fork for desired consistency).
  3. Add slivered onions, sprinkle with salt, and serve.
