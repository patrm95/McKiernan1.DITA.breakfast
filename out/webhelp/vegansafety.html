Saftey Instruction

not only are there hazzards when it comes to cooking, but there are also hazzards that come with selecting ingredients

this list will warn about the potential dangers that could arise when cooking vegan recipes

Saftey steps

  1. Avoid touching hot surfaces, such as the stove top or hot pans
  2. Cut or peel ingredients away from your body to avoid injury
  3. Clean your cooking area, including your tools, such as pots and utensils
  4. Most importantly know the ingredients you are using, as a vegan it is important to use specific ingredinets so make sure you know what you are cooking
