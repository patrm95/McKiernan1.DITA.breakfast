Vegan Pancakes with Tofu Sausage

As a vegan option this recipe requires making the pancakes and vegan sausage seperately

In addition to the ingredients you will also need measuring cups, a food processor, cooking bowls, frying pan, and a stove top

Ingredients for Sausage

  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 3⁄4 cup rolled oats
  • 2 tablespoons whole wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon ground flax seeds
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 teaspoon molasses
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • tablespoons nutritional yeast

Ingredients for Pancakes

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tablespoon cane sugar
  • 2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 1⁄8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup soymilk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Recipe

  1. First begin by making the sausage
  2. Start by stiring together flax and water in a small bowl or cup, set aside.
  3. In a food processor, add dry oats and pulse on high six or seven times, add the rice and pulse a few more times.
  4. Add remaining ingredients including 1 tablespoon of canola oil and flax/water mixture and blend till just mixed.
  5. With dampened hands, form balls the size of a ping pong ball and then flatten into little patties
  6. coat the bottom of a non-stick frying pan with the remaining 1 tablespoon canola oil and heat over medium high.
  7. When the oil is hot, cook the patties on each side until they are browned.
  8. Once done place sausage on warm stove top, and cover to keep warm
  9. Next begin making pancake batter
  10. Begin by setting pan to medium temp on stove top
  11. Next combine the 4 dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl
  12. Add the soy milk and vegetable oil to your mixture (mix until smooth).
  13. Next place a sausage on the skillet and pour one pancakes worth of mixture on top.
  14. Flip carefully when the edges begin to harden or the center bubbles
  15. Repeat until the batter is gone
