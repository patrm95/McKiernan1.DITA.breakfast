Warm and Nutty Cinnamon Quinoa

A stunning berry-studded breakfast Quinoa

the tools needed for this simple 4 serving recipe are measuring cups, a stove top, and stove save pots

Ingredients

  • 1 cup organic 1% low fat milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup organic quinoa,
  • 2 cups fresh blackberries, organic preferred
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup chopped pecans, toasted
  • 4 teaspoons organic agave nectar

Recipe

  1. Combine milk, water and quinoa in a medium saucepan.
  2. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low; cover and simmer 15 minutes or until most of the liquid is absorbed.
  3. Turn off heat; let stand covered 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in blackberries and cinnamon; transfer to four bowls and top with pecans.
  5. Drizzle 1 teaspoon agave nectar over each serving.
  6. While the quinoa cooks, roast the pecans in a 350F degree toaster oven for 5 to 6 minutes or in a dry skillet over medium heat for about 3 minutes.
